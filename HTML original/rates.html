Terms and Conditions:

Room rates

All B&B room rates are per person per night based on two people sharing and include breakfast and VAT.  There is a 2 night minimum stay over a Saturday. A single occupancy surcharge of €40 per night applies. Children (3 years to 15 years inclusive) who share a room with their parents are charged at €20 per child.  A nominal supplement of €10 per night is charged for a travel cot, reservation at time of booking is essential.  An additional cleaning cost of €50 will be applied in the event of damage to towels, linen and/or other furnishings.  Any additional damage to property within Dunowen House will be at cost to the guest.  The tariff and information listed was correct at the time of publication and may be subject to change without notice.

Check-in & check-out

Check-in is between 3pm and 9pm.  Although we will try our best to accommodate all arrivals it is the responsibility of the guest to advise of an arrival after 9pm by prior notification.  Check out on the day of departure is by 11am.

Pets

We allow well-behaved pets by prior agreement to stay at Dunowen House when the house is taken on a self-catering basis. Unfortunately, we cannot accommodate pets when staying on a B&B basis. Pets are strictly not allowed on furniture or in bedrooms and must sleep in a specially designated area.

Smoking

Dunowen House is a strictly non-smoking house.

Cancellations

Regretfully, cancellations made less than 28 days prior to the date of arrival are charged at 75% of the cost of the accommodation unless the room is re-let.  Cancellations within 3 days of the arrival date are to be charged in full.

If rooms are cancelled more than 28 days before the date of arrival, we will refund the deposit, less a €10 administration charge.  We strongly recommend that you take out appropriate cancellation insurance.

Parking

There is ample private parking available.  Please note that parking is entirely at the risk of guests and we cannot accept any liability for loss of or damage to any vehicle or its contents.

Booking a room

To confirm a booking we require a 20% deposit, by credit or debit card. 

To book on-line please click Make a Booking and fill in our Booking Form.  To discuss your holiday requirements and for whole house bookings please call  +353 (0)23 886 9099 or email enquiries@dunowenhouse.ie
