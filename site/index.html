The History of Dunowen House

Originally built in the late 1700’s as the home of ancient Kerry landlords, the Sandes family, Dunowen has had many colourful inhabitants over the years.  Secluded, sheltered and pebbly Sands Cove, a five-minute walk from the front gates, takes its name from these original owners.

A bell tower and bell remain in tact, overlooking the walled garden and orchard. The bell was used to summon the seven gardeners who worked at Dunowen during the late 1800’s.
