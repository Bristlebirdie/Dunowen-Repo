How to Book

Dunowen House is available to rent in its entirety for groups of up to 18 all year round. Outside of high-season periods, guests can stay in one of five luxury suites on a boutique bed and breakfast basis. See rates for both options on our Rates & Availability page.

If you would like to discuss your holiday options personally, please call us on (023) 8869099 or (086) 8351121. Alternatively complete the enquiry form below or email enquiries@dunowenhouse.ie
